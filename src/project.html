This are projects and our works

SANITARY PADS

The OSIM Foundation actively supports local communities, particularly schools, by providing sanitary pads to address the essential menstrual hygiene needs of students, especially girls. Our initiative aims to empower female students, break down barriers to education, and create a supportive learning environment. We collaborate with schools, educators, and community leaders to implement sustainable and culturally sensitive menstrual hygiene programs, prioritizing education and breaking the stigma surrounding menstruation. Our goal is to foster open dialogue and understanding while ensuring uninterrupted access to education.

MATERNAL AND INFANT CARE

OSIM Foundation prioritizes maternal and infant care as crucial elements of our holistic community well-being approach. We implement targeted initiatives to ensure access to essential healthcare and nutrition for mothers and infants. Our programs provide expectant mothers with prenatal education, healthcare, and nutritional support for healthy pregnancies. Postnatally, we support new mothers with guidance on breastfeeding, nutrition, and infant hygiene. Through collaboration with local healthcare professionals, leaders, and volunteers, we create a nurturing environment to enhance the health and development of both mothers and infants.

MALNUTRITION

OSIM Foundation is dedicated to combating malnutrition in local communities by providing essential food services. Our mission is to enhance nutritional well-being through targeted initiatives, ensuring individuals and families have access to nourishing meals for overall health and development. Collaborating with local communities, our sustainable, community-driven programs address specific nutritional challenges. Working closely with community members, we aim to create a supportive environment empowering individuals to overcome malnutrition. Through food services, OSIM Foundation makes a meaningful difference in the lives of those facing malnutrition.
