Osim Foundation is a non profit organization dedicated to enhance maternal and child health, supporting sanitary pad initiatives, nutritional programs, and empowering women in remote communities of Tanzania.

The organization focuses on health education, promoting health-seeking behavior, and addressing various dimensions of poverty through socio-economic empowerment programs in marginalized areas
